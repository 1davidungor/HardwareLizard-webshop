Data Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. This privacy policy explains how SoftwareLizard processes personal data when you use the HardwareLizard website and services.

Legal basis

Personal data is processed in accordance with the applicable legal provisions, in particular the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Austrian Data Protection Act (DSG).

Data processed

- Contact form

When you contact us via the contact form, we process the data you provide (name, e-mail address, message topic, message content) in order to handle your inquiry.

- User accounts (login / sign up)

When you create a user account or log in, we process the data required for account creation and authentication, such as name, e-mail address and password.

- Subscription and checkout

When selecting a subscription plan and completing the checkout process, we process contact and payment-related data necessary to fulfill the contractual relationship.

Technical data

When visiting the website, technical information may be processed automatically, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, date and time of access. This data is used to ensure the security and proper functioning of the website.

Cookies

Cookies may be used on this website to improve usability and provide essential website functions. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

You can restrict or disable cookies via your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit the functionality of the website.

Analytics and usage analysis

We may use analytical tools to evaluate the use of our website and improve our services. These analyses are carried out in an anonymized or aggregated form whenever possible.

Data storage and security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse or destruction.

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the respective purpose or as required by law.

Data transfer to third parties

Personal data will not be transferred to third parties unless this is necessary to fulfill contractual obligations, required by law, or you have expressly consented.

Rights of data subjects

If you believe that the processing of your personal data violates data protection law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority.

Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect legal or technical changes. The current version is published on this website.

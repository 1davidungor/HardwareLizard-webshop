About us - SoftwareLizard & HardwareLizard

Company overview

SoftwareLizard is a one-person software start-up focused on developing lightweight, privacy-conscious tools that help users make better purchasing decisions in the IT hardware market.

Our main product, HardwareLizard, is a browser-based software solution that assists users in identifying competitive hardware deals across multiple online shops.

Our brand forms

Word mark

HardwareLizard is the registered word mark of the product. The name combines the product domain (“Hardware”) with the metaphor of a lizard, symbolizing awareness, adaptability, and fast reaction to changing environments.

Figurative mark (logo)

The HardwareLizard logo represents the brand visually. It is used across the website, browser extension interface, and marketing materials.

Sound mark

HardwareLizard uses a short audio signal as a sound mark. The sound is intended to be used for notifications and confirmations within the software.

Meaning and design rationale

The brand identity of HardwareLizard focuses on clarity, trust, and efficiency. The visual design avoids aggressive colors and uses high contrast combinations to ensure accessibility and readability.

Nizza classification

  • Class 9: Downloadable software and browser extensions
  • Class 35: Price comparison and consumer information services
  • Class 42: Software as a Service (SaaS) and web-based software solutions

Trademark clearance

Prior to designing the brand, trademark searches were conducted using EUIPO eSearch, TMview, and Madrid Monitor to identify potential conflicts with existing trademarks. No identical or confusingly similar trademarks were found in the relevant Nizza classes. A documentation regarding those searches can be found here.

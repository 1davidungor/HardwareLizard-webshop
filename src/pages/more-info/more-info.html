More information about HardwareLizard

HardwareLizard is a browser-based tool that helps you track hardware prices across multiple online shops, identify the best deal over time, and avoid overpaying when the market fluctuates.

Show features Back to home

Who this is for

HardwareLizard is designed for private hardware buyers and small to mid-sized IT teams who purchase hardware regularly and want to make procurement decisions with better market visibility.

The problem we solve

Hardware prices can change quickly due to demand spikes, limited availability, and seasonal promotions. HardwareLizard helps you keep an overview across many shops and price changes so you can buy at the right moment.

What HardwareLizard does

HardwareLizard monitors product listings across supported online shops and normalizes them into comparable entries (e.g.:same SKU, variant, condition). Instead of checking prices manually every day, users maintain a watchlist and let HardwareLizard surface price drops and relevant changes.

Typical workflow

  1. Add a product (or category) to your watchlist.
  2. Choose alert conditions (e.g., “below X€”, “drop by 10%”, “back in stock”).
  3. Review ranked offers with price-history context and shipping information.
  4. Buy when the deal matches your target.

Feature deep dive

Multi-shop comparison

HardwareLizard consolidates offers from multiple dealers into one view. Listings are normalized to reduce noise caused by different naming conventions and optional bundles.

Price history and deal quality signals

Instead of relying on a single “current price”, HardwareLizard compares an offer against recent price history. This helps identify whether a discount is meaningful or just a temporary fluctuation.

Watchlists and alerts

Users set alerts for specific products or configurations. Alerts can be triggered by price thresholds, stock changes, or shipping cost changes (depending on subscription tier).

Smarter alternatives

For higher tiers, HardwareLizard suggests alternative products with similar performance characteristics but better value. This is useful when the originally desired SKU is overpriced or out of stock.

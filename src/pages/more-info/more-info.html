More information about HardwareLizard

HardwareLizard is a browser-based tool that helps you track hardware prices across multiple online shops, identify the best deal over time, and avoid overpaying when the market fluctuates.

View subscriptions Back to home

Who this is for

HardwareLizard is designed for private hardware buyers and small to mid-sized IT teams who purchase hardware regularly and want to make procurement decisions with better market visibility.

The problem we solve

Hardware prices can change quickly due to demand spikes, limited availability, and seasonal promotions. HardwareLizard helps you keep an overview across many shops and price changes so you can buy at the right moment.

Core features

  • Multi-shop price comparison: Compare offers for the same product across multiple dealers.
  • Price history signals: Identify whether a deal is genuinely good relative to recent pricing.
  • Watchlist and alerts: Track products and get notified when prices drop.
  • Plan-based limits: Free/Pro/Premium tiers with increasing utility and flexibility.

Advanced features (higher tiers)

  • Coupon discovery: Assist users in finding applicable discount codes.
  • Alternative suggestions: Recommend similar products with better price-to-performance.
  • E-waste / refurbished sources: Include selected second-hand channels where appropriate.
  • B2B partner offers: Optional integration for exclusive deals for business customers.
